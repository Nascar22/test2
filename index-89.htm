Who are we?

IoDLT (Internet of Distributed Ledger Technology) uses Symbol from NEM to secure, record, and monetize data from a wide array of sources, including IoT devices.

 

Properly Manage Data

IoDLT believes data, as it stands, is improperly managed. The advent of the blockchain made it much more possible to verify and secure the integrity of massive amounts of data around us.

IoDLT focuses on the relevant data from a wide variety of sources, such as IoT.  This translates into smarter, cost effective, more efficient data management, IoT, and incentivization systems for businesses. 

Blazing Fast Performance

Utilizing Symbol’s API interfaces, our IoT and client-side solutions can efficiently read and write from the blockchain.

Scalable Architecture

We are able to configure a Symbol blockchain to work for your specific workloads.

Highly Secure & Reliable

We are proud to offer secure, end-to-end blockchain and IoT solutions. Whether you are a small business, medical office, or enterprise, IoDLT can scale to meet your demands.

Industries we cater to.

IoDLT is able to deliver end-to-end solutions to a multitude of different industries.

 

 

Industrial

IoDLT provides integration solutions for industrial and manufacturing machinery. These solutions utilize IoT and blockchain for the end-to-end tracking in an industrial setting.

Healthcare

 IoDLT offers HIPAA-compliant EHR management tools that utilize blockchain technology to the healthcare industry. IoDLT also offers IoMT, or Internet of Medical Things, for the purpose of improving healthcare provider worflows and the quality of service for doctor-to-patient processes. 

Energy

IoDLT provides IoT solutions pertaining to the renewable energy industry. The data gathered by our IoT devices can be used for analytics, energy trading, and more.

Agricultural

Through stationary IoT devices, farmers can gather in-depth insights on their crops’ mineralization, soil moisture, and crop status. This information is securely stored on the blockchain, where access is controlled to only the appropriate parties.

      Dash Investment Foundation (DIF) Invests in CrayPay and Announces 155,000 Merchant Instant Savings App for Dash

      We are proud to announce an exciting collaboration that will soon enable Dash spending at over 155,000 merchant locations across the United States. The Dash Investment Foundation (DIF) recently invested in CrayPay, a loyalty payment platform that enables users to earn instant savings at a variety of national chains.

      CrayPay is the world’s first universal loyalty platform with instant rewards. Consumers get instant savings at over 155,000 locations and 75+ national US retailer websites. Current merchant partners include American Eagle, Autozone, Best Buy, Chili's, CVS, Domino's, DoorDash, GAP, Lowe's, Staples, and over 200 more national retailers and restaurants.
      CrayPay is the first mobile payment platform to combine instant savings on everyday purchases with easy to use geolocation technology. CrayPay’s unique attributes provide an opportunity to be the leader in proximity to mobile payments and, in the process, capture a significant share of the multi-trillion dollar mobile payment market.
      In coordination with the DIF’s investment, Dash Core Group has reached an agreement to create a white-label version of CrayPay’s instant savings application exclusively for Dash users. Thanks to the low transaction fees of the Dash network, the Dash-enabled version of CrayPay’s app will seek to offer more merchants and greater savings than is typically available through the regular CrayPay application.

      “CrayPay is the perfect solution to provide Dash users with a convenient method of spending their Dash at a huge number of merchant locations, through an application that delivers a great purchase experience. Not only will users have access to more spending opportunities for their Dash, but they can save significantly more than they could paying with a cash-back credit card, providing a strong incentive for using Dash as often as possible.”

      Ryan Taylor, CEO, Dash Core Group

      Dash users will benefit from the significant savings opportunities offered on the platform, and the large number of locations supported. Savings will increase the more the Dash community uses the platform and reaches specific milestones.

      “We’re excited to work with the Dash community to bring our loyalty program to Dash users. Dash’s InstantSend technology eliminates chargeback risks, which enables CrayPay to instantly authorize payment to our merchant partners, so Dash really is the best option for instant low-cost payments on our platform.”

      Marshall Greenwald, CEO, CrayPay

      “CrayPay has built a high-quality business that we’re thrilled to have in the portfolio. Not only does CrayPay have tremendous potential as a business, but there is obvious value for Dash users.”

      Darren Tapp, DIF Supervisor, Dash Investment Foundation 

      Get instant savings at the brands you love, every time at https://www.craypay.com.

      MARK MASON

      Communications and Business Relations Manager at Dash

      About Dash

      Dash is digital cash designed to offer financial freedom to everyone. Dash allows millions of individuals across the globe to carry out instantly re-spendable and secure transactions, with near-zero fees. Built to support real-life use cases, Dash is the leading decentralized payment solution: it is accepted at merchants globally, accessible via 1,600+ ATMs and 100+ exchanges worldwide. Dash has pioneered groundbreaking features including a two-tier network with incentivized nodes and decentralized project governance (Masternodes), instantly settled payments (InstantSend), instantly immutable blockchain (ChainLocks) and more. The Dash Network’s development is supported by Dash Core Group, the largest organization funded by the Dash Treasury, which supports and nurtures the continued worldwide development and integration of Dash. For more information, visit https://www.dash.org/ or follow the organization on Twitter @DashPay.

      About CrayPay

      CrayPay is the world’s first universal loyalty platform with instant rewards. Consumers get instant savings at over 155,000 locations and 75+ national US retailer websites. Current merchant partners include American Eagle, Autozone, Best Buy, Chili's, CVS, Domino's, DoorDash, GAP, Lowe's, Staples, and over 200 more national retailers and restaurants. For more information visit https://www.craypay.com/.

      About Dash Investment Foundation

      The Dash Investment Foundation (DIF) is the world’s first ownerless and memberless investment fund. It was incorporated on March 21st 2019 as a Cayman Islands foundation company limited by guarantee and is completely controlled by Dash’s decentralized Masternode Network. Dash Investment Foundation is tasked with strengthening the Dash network through investment operations. By this the foundation creates a bridge between the network protocol and the legacy financial and legal systems. This opens new possibilities for entrepreneurs and the Dash network to partner and mutually benefit from the funding available through the Dash network via Dash Investment Foundation. For more information, visit https://www.dashinvests.org/ or follow the organization on Twitter @DashInvests.

Symbol Launch: The Process

Because the technology stack between NEM NIS1 and Symbol is not compatible, the Symbol blockchain will be introduced in parallel with NIS1. In order to give XEM holders agency through the migration, the token allocation of the XYM tokens will be determined by an Opt-in process. By opting-in, XEM holders will obtain an XYM balance equal to that of their XEM balance at the time of Snapshot.

It will also be possible to migrate multi-signature account configuration and any root namespaces.

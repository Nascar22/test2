The Art of Skeletonising

In 1995 the complex 300-part masterpiece Opus Chronograph advanced off the skeletonising chapter at Chronoswiss. The new version bears a modern geometry with a 41-millimetre diameter and shorter lugs.

In the limited edition of the emblematic chronograph, the subdials showing date, chronograph hours and minutes and small seconds add a little colour to a horological opus from Lucerne.

A new Era in Skeletonising

With the aptly named SkelTec, Chronoswiss proudly presents a radically progressive skeleton timepiece featuring a 166 part hand-wound movement exclusively developed in the company´s atelier in Lucerne.

Modern Mechanical at its best

The new Open Gear ReSec represents the perfect symbiosis of horological know-how, nonconformist design, modern materials and classical artisanal mastery.

Inimitability around the clock

Made to last: For longevity and perpetual fascination, our hand-guilloched dials are coated with modern materials, resulting in a deep blue in this case.

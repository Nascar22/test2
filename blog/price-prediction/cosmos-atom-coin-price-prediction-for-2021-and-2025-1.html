WHAT IS COSMOS (ATOM)?

Joe Kwon founded Cosmos cryptocurrency. He was a former programmer at Amazon. Joe Kwon worked with Ethan Buchman to build the Cosmos (Atom). Ethan Buchman aims to provide the community with various options for engaging in consensus-building across a wide range of industries. At the same time, Jae Kwon is the creator of the Tendermint protocol. He is passionate about promoting the Tendermint consensus algorithm, which he positions as the most efficient consensus algorithm that seeks maximum speed and scalability.

Together they created Tendermint in 2017, which was created to replace proof-of-work as the consensus algorithm for blockchains, which eventually turned into a company, birthing Cosmos(Atom), in march 2019, an initial coin offering (ICO). They managed to raise $17 million in less than 30 minutes of the ICO, and one ATOM was sold for $0.1

The Cosmos (Atom) goal is to create an ‘internet of blockchains’ that resolves both scalability and interoperability issues in the blockchain. The Cosmos network can interact with other cryptocurrencies and applications, which other blockchain networks cannot achieve.

ZCash (ZEC) Features

ZCash (ZEC) has some hardcore features and capabilities mentioned below.

Zcash has low-fee transactions that support fast transactions with a low price of .0001 ZEC. This is mainly why Zcash is supported on a large number of top wallets and exchanges. For example, Atomic wallet.

ZCash provides address and transaction privacy, which is of two types: shielded and transparent. In shielded one, addresses and transactions between shielded addresses do not reveal itself, including address, the amount of the transaction, or the contents of the encrypted memo field. In transparent, addresses and transactions between the transparent addresses are published on the ZCash blockchain just as the case in Bitcoin addresses are visible.

Encrypted memos is a memo field available for shielded transactions. It permits the sender to fill in the necessary info of the receiver, which is entirely encrypted. It is a must for exchanging along with messages and instructions, or to establish conformity with the Bank Secrecy Act “Travel” rule.

Multisignature transactions ZCash encourages transactions requiring multiple signatures. That means few transactions may demand two or more parties to agree upon a transaction before funds are made available. Copy that, multi-signature transactions are transparent ones and can not be shielded.

Payment disclosure it’s a protocol in which the sender or receiver involved in a transaction may disclose the specific details of the transaction to the 3rd party as per the requirements needed for visibility, proof, or conformity. Anyone may disclose a transaction value and memo without any access to the other user’s address. This protocol is currently under attestation to be launched soon in the future.

What is ZCash (ZEC)?

Zcash is a digital asset, cryptocurrency, that was launched by Zerocoin Electric Coin Company on 9th September 2016. This is the first example of a cryptocurrency marrying the concepts of blockchain technology with Zk-Snarks.

ZCash company CEO Zooko Wilcox introduced ZCash (ZEC) and pointed the project’s intentions to create a coin not having drawbacks as Bitcoin has. The plan was actually to provide a better alternative. Having this goal in mind, the team set up ZCash with a science similar to Bitcoin with extra features having semi-transparent options and high-security set-up.

ZCash (ZEC) is a virtual money that host’s decentralized blockchain technology, where users enjoy their transactions freely with security and anonymity. In terms of currency, ZCash is much similar to Bitcoin, including features like open-source and having a harsh limit of 21 million coins. Still, it has a vast difference in terms of maintaining the confidentiality level and compatibility that it provides using trailblazing cryptography.

Zero-knowledge proofs are the core science behind ZCash. They make transactions successful without disclosing any info about the amount and the parties involved. ZCash utilizes specific zero-knowledge proofs known to be as zk-SNARKs (zero-knowledge succinct non-interactive arguments of knowledge).

What is Pundi X (NPXS)?

Pundi price prediction 2020. It’s a cryptocurrency project from Singapore. Its main goal is providing a cryptocurrency service with wide adoption and simplicity for everyday use. The project currently aims at the South-East Asia market. The reason is financial underdevelopment: 73% of the SEA population don’t have a bank account.

Their key product designed for improving crypto adoption is XPOS. It is a point-of-sale terminal allowing crypto payments in any kind of B2C business: grocery stores, restaurants, gyms and so on. XPOS looks much like a simple cashier terminal but works with the Ethereum platform allowing quick and easy payments in crypto. The company is planning to ship 100 000 devices in the first three years.

Pundi X is based on the Ethereum platform. The project’s native token is called ‘Pundi X’ (the ticker is NPXS). It is an ERC-20 token designed to support the ecosystem and help it to develop.

The project was first announced on March 8, 2018. The presale started in September 2018 and finished on the ICO date — January 21, 2019. Since then, NPXS got listed on 24 major exchanges.

Pundi X (NPXS) Technical Features

Pundi X has a quite sophisticated distribution policy. Upon the ICO, the purchasers only got 30% of the amount they had previously bought. The rest is enrolled monthly according to smart contracts users must sign on a regular basis. But the main requirement of these contracts is that a user must hold the amount through the whole 3-year period. On top of that, NPXS is not mined, it only can be bought. Technically it works with the Proof-of-Stake algorithm but it cannot be earned by staking.

As was said, Pundi X works on the Ethereum platform, but it also involves different financial institutions for crypto and fiat conversions and transactions. In order to gain the support of fiat financial organizations, Pundi X runs a policy of transparency. As a part of it, everyone who wants to sign in the Pundi’s ecosystem must go through a KYC control procedure. On top of that, the platform is open for developers, so anyone can make an app for XPOS for NPXS charges. Also, NPXS is the native token for in-app transactions.

Also, it’s curious that Pundi X is one of the few cryptocurrencies that pick offline as the main promotion space.

Pundi X Announces A Blockchain Smartphone

One of the loudest Pundi X news is the announcement of their BOB Smartphone. BOB stands for ‘Blok on Blok’ and means that this phone is fully powered with blockchain technologies. Pundi’s representatives showed the first prototype on the IFA trade show in Berlin that took place from the 4th to the 9th of September 2019.

Ingenico and Pundi X Made a Partnership

One of the biggest fintech giants Ingenico reached a partnership agreement with Pundi X. The point is to make crypto transactions possible all over the world with high speed and ease of use. The two companies are working on the integration of Pundi’s POS-soft with Ingenico’s devices.

Pundi X Gets Integrated With Samsung Blockchain Wallet

South Korea’s electronics giant made an agreement with Pundi X in August-September 2019. The main point is joint work on the Samsung Blockchain Wallet — an official pre-installed crypto wallet for Samsung mobile devices.

Pundi X (NPXS) Price Prediction Verdict

Before we make any conclusions it must be clarified that this article is not financial advice. Before making an investment, make your own research.

First of all, we would like to point to some factors that make an impact on the Pundi X price.

Positive Factors

  • High level of support from community which resulted in early listing on several major exchanges like Binance and Kraken.
  • Partnership agreements with several financial companies, both crypto and non-crypto: Stellar, NEM, Qtum, Ingenico.
  • Solid marketing guidelines and goals of improving mass crypto adoption in the business community.

Negative Factors

  • Numerous strong competitors.
  • The project is based not only on decentralized solutions but on centralized ones: hardware production and support, client base management etc.
  • The main purpose as a payment method, which means NPXS price might not grow to high levels.

However, the Pundi X project seems promising. It has high goals, a clear focus on their target audience and interesting solutions in the crypto segment. This all makes Pundi X a quite curious option of investment that may blast in the near future.

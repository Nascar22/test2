Theta Price Prediction 2021 – Theta Market Prediction and Price Verdict

What is Theta?

Video streaming is all around us and in high demand. A good portion of internet bandwidth is now used to stream videos. This industry keeps growing and will only continue to grow in the coming years. However, the current centralized video streaming platforms have various inadequacies plunging their services. That is what Theta has come to solve.

Theta is a decentralized platform that is introducing video streaming and video content into the blockchain space. Theta has the vision to provide – Next-generation video delivery powered by everyone. Steve Chen, a co-founder of YouTube, who is also part of the Theta team, said:

“Theta’s innovation will disrupt today’s online video industry much like YouTube did to traditional videos back in 2005. One of our biggest challenges has been the high costs of delivering videos, and this is only getting bigger with higher quality video streams. I’m excited to be part of the next evolution of the streaming space.”

To achieve its vision of delivering seamless, high-quality streams up to HD and 4K resolutions at low cost, Theta is looking to bring everyone’s resources together. How? They plan to employ the unused storage and bandwidth of users on the network. Thus, there will be enough room for videos of very high resolutions while participants receive rewards for their contributions. As said, video streaming platforms like YouTube, Netflix, and Live.ly continue to grow. Major social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter even have live video options. However, these videos become blurry most times, especially in developing countries where internet service is poor.

Theta team is innovating a lasting solution for video delivery using distributed blockchain technology. Thus, Theta Labs have been able to build the first Decentralized Streaming Network. Theta Labs is the parent company in charge of the Theta blockchain. Jieyi Long and Mitch Liu co-founded Theta Labs in 2018. Jieyi Long has multiple experiences in gaming, virtual reality, design automation, and distributed systems. He also holds patents in virtual reality, video streaming, and blockchain technology. Likewise, Liu has multiple experiences in the entertainment industry, including co-founding THETA.tv. The very first decentralized application built on the Theta network is THETA.tv.

The Theta blockchain has two native tokens, Theta (THETA) and Theta Fuel (TFUEL). THETA exists as a governance token and, at launch in 2018, was a token on the ERC-20 network. Theta held a one-year-long private token sale which ended in 2018. The company was able to raise $20 million during the initial sale. However, Theta now runs its main net, and all THETA tokens use the network.

Meanwhile, Theta Fuel (TFUEL) powers the internal economy of the Theta blockchain. The Theta blockchain uses the Proof-of-Stake financial incentive scheme to ensure its users participate in governance activities. This way, the network is secured by participants and holders of the Theta token. Also, the blockchain utilizes the Byzantine Fault Tolerance (BFT) mechanism to balance its security. The multi-level BFT also ensures faster transactions, and the network can process up to 1000 transactions per second.

To easily calculate user engagement with live videos, the network relies on the Proof-of-Engagement protocol. This protocol provides transparent reports of users who have watched a video and rewards them. Hence, advertisers, creators, and viewers can efficiently measure their video streaming engagement.

Theta Price Chart

Theta has existed since 2018. However, it became tradable on Binance in April 2019. At that time, Theta coin price was $0.135 before dumping below 10 cents a month after Binance’s listing. It was able to recover and kept on increasing in price. Theta’s current price is $4.30, and its all-time high is $4.8. In March 2020, Theta price USD was 0.03977, and this price is the coin’s all-time low. Theta coin price is still on the rise, and possibly, it will continue to reach new all-time highs.

Market Prediction for Theta (THETA) Price 2021

Theta’s current price, like other cryptocurrencies, is moving with the current market trend. Its all-time high is a recent price, so we may continue to have more highs if the market proves bullish. Going back, Theta price as of January 2018 was around $0.2, and it did drop significantly to as low as $0.039. However, since the Binance listing, Theta coin price continues to climb higher. Stats from various Theta news shows that the Theta project has a substantial audience in the cryptocurrency market. Theta ranks 18 among over 8,700 on the list of cryptocurrencies on CoinMarketCap. According to Theta’s current price chart, the coin has formed support levels at $1.84 and $2.85. Since 2021, the Theta coin price has not broken any of these pivot points.

Currently, Theta’s market capitalization and fully diluted cap are $4,301,936,592. THETA’s daily trading volume is over $190 million, and more than 90,000 traders have it on their watchlist. THETA coin has a circulating supply, total and maximum supply of 1 billion (1,000,000,000) tokens. THETA serves as a governance token and is distributed among the Theta network participants, including the Theta team. The Theta network also powers decentralized applications on its smart contract platform. However, developers power transactions using Theta Fuel (TFUEL). TFUEL is another token of Theta with a 5 billion total supply.

The blockchain-based video streaming platform has some big plans to execute in the 2021 calendar year. Theta news includes some impressive roadmap the company has for 2021:

  •  The launch of a decentralized exchange on the Theta network in quarter one of 2021
  •  Launch of Theta mainnet 3.0 in quarter two
  •  Theta official app for Ledger and Trezor wallet also in Q2 of 2021
  •  Release of Version 2 of the Edgecast Decentralized Streaming App (Dapp) – Q3 2021
  •  Version 2 of the Advanced Smart Contract Developer – Q4 2021

Theta co-founder Mitch Liu also laid out another plan for this year – the Edgecast Node platform. This platform will deliver local ingest and encoding of streams, fully decentralized streaming, chat, and event notifications. Similarly, Theta Edgecast will be the world’s first decentralized streaming Dapp for end-to-end live streaming and video delivery. According to Liu, there is a rapid growth in Theta.tv and the native cryptocurrencies of the Theta network.

Another exciting announcement from Theta news is the Google Chrome extension for Theta Wallet. This update is a substantial achievement because Theta has just partnered with the world’s biggest search engine. By April 20, Theta is expected to launch the THETA MAINNET 3.0, which in their roadmap is set for Q2 of 2021. This new network upgrade will introduce TFuel staking and uptime mining and burning. Participants who want to stake TFUEL will have to download the THETA Edge Node.

Announcements, upgrades, and improvements like these are enough to make THETA price skyrocket in 2021. Theta news is impressive, and this is enough to excite investors. No wonder THETA is on over 90,000 watchlists on CoinMarketCap. Theta is a promising blockchain project, and Theta coin price may go beyond expectations in 2021.

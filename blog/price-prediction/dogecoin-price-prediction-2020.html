What is Dogecoin (DOGE)

Dogecoin (also known as DOGE) is a Litecoin-based cryptocurrency mined with the Proof-of-Work algorithm. It is designed as an alternative to major coins such as Bitcoin with an emphasis on easier mining and better accessibility. DOGE has the Japanese Shiba Inu dog breed as a logo and mascot which became popular thanks to an old meme — it was picked as a symbol of friendly and fun marketing policy.

Dogecoin was created in 2013 by programmers Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer. They aimed to invent an altcoin setting apart from Bitcoin. The initial Dogecoin mining complexity was set to a 1-minute level in order to provide better adoption for big demography groups. As a result, the project became a sort of folklore currency: users in social media use it to send tips to each other for creating and distributing media content. DOGE is also known for participation in charity activities, supporting the Jamaican bobsleigh Olympic team and NASCAR racer Josh Wise.

Dogecoin (DOGE) Market Features

As of this writing, the Dogecoin current price is $0,002656 or 0,00000031 BTC. The all-time high was $0,018773 on Jan 7, 2018. The all-time low DOGE price was $0,000085 after the cryptomarket crash on May 7, 2015. Initially, the Dogecoin blockchain was designed to have a total issue of 100 billion coins, but later the limit was removed. As a result, there are 122 billion DOGE in circulation.

Dogecoin price has been fluctuating in a sideway since the beginning of 2018. It’s remarkable that the fun-driven Dogecoin has a quite stable price even when the whole market experiences roller coaster rides. DOGE currently takes the 30th place in the world cryptocurrency ratings.

Dogecoin (DOGE) News

Dogecoin news has been quite positive for the last two years. Experts, traders and major portals mark that Dogecoin price has significant support which leads to market growth. On top of that, DOGE is getting more and more support from world-known crypto exchanges and services.

Dogecoin (DOGE) Gets Listed on Binance US

One of the latest and most important news is that DOGE/USD pair is now listed on the major US-based exchange Binance US. At the moment, the exchange website shows $0.0025993 as the last Dogecoin price.

Previously, DOGE was listed on the main Binance exchange in July. Right after that DOGE price rose by 30%.

Dogecoin (DOGE) Releases a Bugfix Patch

Dogecoin Reddit community reports that the major bugfix is out. It helps with the backward compatibility and makes transactions go even smoother. Improving the blockchain always has a positive impact on market positions and Dogecoin price should be rising thanks to this update.

Dogecoin Gets Listed on Huobi

Earlier this year, in April DOGE appeared on another major exchange — Huobi. That was the point when people started saying that the project is now in the major league and Dogecoin price might grow.

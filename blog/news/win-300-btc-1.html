To win $300 BTC, you need to check the words in the BIP 39 word list below and match them with the picture in our Twitter where we hid the mnemonic with the prize.

When you collect all words and match the mnemonic, you need to open the wallet with the prize and send it to your BTC address. Check our guide on how to do recovery then.

How to get the prize

  1. Save your mnemonic and BTC-address from Atomic wallet that you use beforehand!
  2. Click on Restore to open the wallet with $300 BTC.

3. Click on Continue, paste the mnemonic we hid in the pictures, and click on Restore at the bottom.

4. Set new Password and confirm it. That’s it

5. As soon as you open the wallet and send the prize to your address, recover your wallet by mnemonic following these steps again.

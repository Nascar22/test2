How to import Atomic Wallet key in Metamask

A simple strategy with steps on how to import Atomic Wallet key in Metamask. Afterwards your MetaMask with the keys from Atomic will be activated.

Step 1: Head to MetaMask website through Google Chrome (or other applicable) browser and click on Get Chrome Extension.

Step 2: Add MetaMask to Chrome In the settings of the browser, download the file to set it up.

Step 3: Once the file is downloaded, you should enter the MetaMask page in Chrome automatically. Otherwise, follow the instructions at the pop-up window. Get started.

Step 4: Click on Create a Wallet. 

Step 5: Agree with Privacy Policy regulations and proceed to the page with the generated secret phrase to back up and restore your account. Create a strong password, read and agree with the Terms of Use. Save your backup phrase in a safe place and confirm it in order at the next window.

Step 6: If all is correct, you’ll get the confirmation, just click on the button All Done. Keep your seed phrase safe, it’s your responsibility! 

Step 7: You’ll open the MetaMask interface. Click on the Account 1 Logo at the upper menu and choose Import Account. Paste your ETH private key from Atomic wallet to attach the wallet. 

Step 8: That’s it! Your MetaMask with the keys from Atomic is activated! Now you’re all set to get claiming a free domain!

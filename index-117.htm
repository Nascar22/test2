SOLUTIONS

Digital Carbon Ecosystem Solution

For Companies Interested in Carbon Reduction

VeChain's Digital Carbon Ecosystem is a ground-breaking blockchain and IoT powered carbon reduction platform which utilizes economic incentives to encourage both individual and enterprises to participate in the reduction of carbon emissions.

Consumer Confidence Index Platform

For Manufacturers of Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG)

As a blockchain-based digital authentication solution designed for retailing, PSCIP's mathematical model generates a unique confidence index for each product according to data on VeChainThor blockchain, all collected and verified by DNV.

Automotive Passport Solution

For Manufacturers of Automobiles and other OEMs

With the Automobile Solution, VeChain assigns each newly manufactured vehicle a unique VeChain ID and a digital passport that covers the entire lifecycle of the vehicle. This solution, which is not only useful for automotive makers but any Original Equipment Manufacturer, empowers brands to ensure auto part authenticity and share unique vehicle data with multiple stake-holders.

Retail Solution

For Manufacturers of High-value Retail Products and FMCG

VeChain uses blockchain and IoT to implement the traceability of retail products' entire lifecycle management. End consumers can check and verify a product's information via the VeChain Pro mobile app (or client-integrated SDK). The retail solution can also contribute to effective channel management, after-sales service, and convenient financial services such as insurance for high value goods.

Logistics Solution

For Companies with Complex Logistics, Inventory, and Warehousing Needs

The logistics industry solution enables users to record immutable and secure data throughout the transportation process, making it possible to create new logistics and business models. In addition, various digital value-added services can be provided based on the data stored on the blockchain.

Liquefied Natural Gas Solution

For Manufacturers and Importers of Liquefied Natural Gas

VeChain and third party partnered to create a track and trace solution for liquiefied natural gas (LNG). The core data generated during the transportation, storage, and online-transaction of LNG is collected and stored on the VeChainThor blockchain, enabling information sharing between multiple parties and improving efficiency with regards to classification standards, weighing practices, and transportation processes.

Digital Content Distribution Solution

For Creators and Distributors of Digital Content

The Digital Content Distribution Solution, which stores important data related to copyright registration and transaction, and content distribution on the VeChainThor blockchain, makes intellectual property traceable. On top of immutability, openness and transparency, the solution aims to reduce third parties, and to achieve peer-to-peer distribution, so that content circulation and transaction can be faster and more transparent.

Electronic Document Deposit Solution

For Enterprises and Governments

VeChain has developed a document management solution that verifies digitized certificates, licenses, and contracts stored on the VeChainThor blockchain. The system eliminates the malicious act of counterfeiting paper-based certificates, bills, contracts, deeds, and other administrative documents for governments and financial services providers.

Agriculture Solution

For Farms and Ranches

VeChain offers a big data platform designed for agriculture which keeps track of all agriculture-related data generated during planting or breeding, production, processing, packaging, sales and distribution. With proper authorization, the data on the VeChainThor blockchain can be shared among all or specific participants for analysis, facilitating the development of smart agriculture.

VECHAIN TOOLCHAIN

VeChain's Blockchain-as-a-Service ("BaaS") platform is called ToolChain. ToolChain is a comprehensive blockchain platform offering diverse services including: product lifecycle management, supply chain process control, data deposit, data certification, and process certification. With ToolChain, any sized business, no matter how large or small, can utilize blockchain technology to further enhance brand perception and value as well as to expand into new business models.

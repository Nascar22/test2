Hardware Wallets

Hardware wallets are security devices designed keep cryptographic keys safe from hackers and thieves. Hardware wallets use random number generators, encrypted key storage, PIN numbers and passwords to offer the highest level of security for your digital currencies.

Trezor
Trezor builds hardware wallets with open source software and firmware. Designed for secure storage of cryptographic private keys, these wallets make secure payments possible without ever exposing your private keys to a potentially compromised computer.
Purchase Options
Usage Instructions

Ledger
Ledger offers a range of hardware wallets targeting different needs for storing cryptographic assets and securing digital payments. Built around a secure chip, these multi-currency wallets ensure optimal security.
Purchase Options

KeepKey
KeepKey is a hardware wallet for secure storage of cryptographic keys. KeepKey works with the wallet software on your computer by taking over the management of private key generation, private key storage, and transaction signing.
Purchase Options

SafePal
SafePal is a secure and user-friendly hardware wallet designed for the masses. Adopting multiple layers of security schemes and a intuitive user interface, SafePal enables users to store, transfer and trade digital currencies in their wallet in the easiest and most secure way.
Purchase Options

D'CENT
D’CENT wallets are a new generation of convenient cold storage. Features such as a built-in fingerprint scanner for fast transaction signing, on-device secure private key generation and an integrated secure element give you confidence in the security of your transactions.
Purchase Options

Cobo Vault
The Cobo Vault is the first hardware wallet that meets US military standard MIL-STD-810G with IP68 waterproof rating and IK9 certification. It is completely air-gapped and includes a bank-grade encryption chip and self-destruct mechanism.
Purchase Options

Ellipal Titan
Ellipal Titan is the world's first fully metal sealed air-gapped cold wallet. It cannot be connected to any connections such as USB, Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, and operates entirely using scannable QR codes, fully protecting you against remote and network attacks.
Purchase Options

NGRAVE
Built to operate completely offline, the NGRAVE ZERO claims to be the most secure hardware wallet in the world. Also available is the NGRAVE GRAPHENE, a backup solution made from Stainless Steel 316, to keep your secret keys safe.

Text (SMS) Wallets

Text wallets (or SMS wallets) allow users without smartphones or internet access to transact in Dash using text messages on simple feature phones.

Dash Text
Dash Text is an SMS wallet service exclusive to Dash, available in the US, Venezuela, Colombia, Spain and Brazil. The service is also available via the Telegram chat application. It enables users to seamlessly and securely send and receive Dash by texting, no downloads or internet required.
Dash Text Website

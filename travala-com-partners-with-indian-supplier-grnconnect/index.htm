Travala.com Expands Partnership with Indian Travel Supplier – GRNConnect

04/03/2020 1 min read

A strategic partnership expansion to help Travala.com extend it’s presence into the world’s second most populated country with 1.35 billion citizens 

 

Based on recent technical upgrades to Travala.com we are pleased to announce the addition of member rates to our strategic partnership with GRNConnect — an Indian based accommodation supplier.

 

Headquartered in New Delhi, GRNConnect has expanded quickly after 24 years of operation with the presence in over 19 countries. The company currently boasts a B2B portfolio of over 500,000 hotels with a very strong presence in their home country of India.

 

The Partnership

 

India is the world’s second most populated country and experiencing a period of unprecedented growth in the travel and tourism industry, this expansion of our partnership with GRNConnect gives Travala.com an even stronger strategic presence in this dynamic market by adding special member rates. Users that have created an account on Travala.com can now enjoy these member rates.

 

Travala.com offers users up to 40% discounts and 2% loyalty rewards on over 2,000,000+ hotels and accommodations worldwide. We currently accept over 25 other cryptocurrencies as payment as well as Credit/Debit cards and Paypal. In addition to unbeatable prices via its Best Price Guarantee, Travala.com Smart members can enjoy additional discounts, loyalty rewards for eligible bookings made on the platform via its native utility token AVA.

 

 

About Travala.com: 
Founded in 2017, Travala.com is the leading cryptocurrency-friendly hotel and accommodation booking service with 2,000,000+ properties in 230 countries. We are a champion of cryptocurrency adoption, accepting over 25 leading cryptocurrencies in addition to traditional payment methods. The Travala.com value proposition is bolstered by AVA. As the native cryptocurrency of the platform, AVA can be used for payments, receiving and redeeming loyalty rewards, discounts and staking, among several other use cases For more information, visit: www.travala.com
